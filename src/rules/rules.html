War

Rules

The goal of the game is to win all 52 cards

The Deal

The deck is divided evenly, with each stack receiving 26 cards face down. The stacks are on the sides of the screen with the players hand on the right.

The Play

Click on your stack of cards and a card from each stack is turned face up at the same time and the higher card takes both cards. They get put on the bottom of the winners stack

If the cards are the same rank, it is War. Another set of cards are turned over and the player with the higher card takes all 4 of the cards in play

Score

The game ends when someone has won all of the cards
